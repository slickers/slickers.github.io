PT. Astha Berkat Bersama is a global industry supplier that located in Jakarta-Indonesia, with branches in BATANG, SEMARANG and SURABAYA.

Our company has served many construction site, mining and oil-gas companies all over Indonesia. We provide many construction materials, equiptment and accessories to support them.

We are well-known not just as a supplier; we trully capable to handling fast response and imported materials. We have specialty in RIGGING, LIFTING, MECHANICAL-ELECTRICAL, SAFETY, WELDING, PIPING and RAW MATERIAL.

All of our products are supplied with Material Certificate, Origin Certificate amd Lifting Certificate. For special product, we also provide Calibration Certificate.
